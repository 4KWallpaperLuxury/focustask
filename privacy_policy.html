Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Focus Tasks (the "App") is developed and maintained by GUNGSA STUDIO. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

🔒 Focus Tasks is designed with a privacy-first approach. We do NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

2. Information We Collect NONE

Focus Tasks operates entirely offline. All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Crash reports

Your tasks, preferences, PIN codes, and all app data remain exclusively on your device.

3. Data Storage

All data created within Focus Tasks is stored locally using:

  • Room Database — for tasks, notifications, badges, and user profile
  • DataStore Preferences — for settings such as language, theme, and PIN configuration
📱 Your data never leaves your device. If you uninstall the App, all data will be permanently deleted.

4. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) — To send task reminders and alerts. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time.
  • Exact Alarms (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM) — To schedule precise task reminders.
  • Boot Completed (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED) — To restore scheduled reminders after device restart.

These permissions are used solely for local reminder functionality. No data is transmitted externally.

5. Third-Party Services

Focus Tasks does not integrate any third-party services such as:

  • Analytics tools (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services
  • Social media SDKs

6. Children's Privacy

Focus Tasks does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since the App does not collect any data at all, it is safe for users of all ages.

7. Security

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • PIN Lock — Optional 4-digit PIN protection for app access
  • Local-only storage — No cloud synchronization, no external data transfer
  • No internet required — The App functions fully offline

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
